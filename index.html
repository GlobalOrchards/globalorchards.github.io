Who We Are

Founded on principles of quality and sustainability, Global Orchards connects premium produce growers with retailers across Canada.

Our Story

Global Orchards was established with a vision to revolutionize the produce supply chain, cutting unnecessary steps between grower and retailer to deliver fresher products with less waste.

Today, we're proud to work directly with orchards, farms, and growing operations throughout British Columbia, the Okanagan Valley, and international partners to bring exceptional produce to Canadian retailers.

Our commitment to sustainability drives everything we do, from eco-friendly packaging to optimized logistics that reduce our carbon footprint.
